# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Admins, Supervisors, and Technicians at Art of Baking (AOB). Technicians in particular will use this in factory/manufacturing conditions where they need to perform deep analysis, view charts, and check historical predictive data before acting.

## Product Purpose
A machine maintenance management system to handle machines, spare parts inventory, PM (Preventive Maintenance) scheduling, and predictive analytics using AI. Success means clear traceability, efficient maintenance operations, and reducing machine downtime.

## Brand Personality
Utilitarian, dense, function-over-form. It should prioritize information density and functional utility. 

## Anti-references
- Old-fashioned, clunky interfaces where accessing and editing data is difficult and tedious.
- Consumer-like whitespace that wastes screen real estate.
- Generic, un-optimized layouts that slow down workflow.

## Design Principles
1. **Data over Decoration**: Prioritize clear, dense information display (charts, history) to enable deep analysis.
2. **Frictionless Entry**: Ensure editing and accessing data is fast and straightforward, avoiding old-school clunky forms.
3. **Contextual Clarity**: Display the right information at the right time to supervisors and technicians so they can act confidently.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Needs high contrast mode and large touch targets to accommodate factory floor conditions where lighting or device usage might not be ideal.
